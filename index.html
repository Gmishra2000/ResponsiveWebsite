Meet the Team

Women Rise is the latest art project by the artist, Maliha Abidi. She is a Pakistani-American published author and visual artist. Her work focuses on advocating for social justice including women’s rights, gender equality, girl’s education and fighting for the marginalised communities. Maliha has been sharing st​ories of incredible women through her art and illustrated books for over 9 years. She has worked with amazing organisations such as UN, Global Goals, Malala Fund, Adobe, Google, and many others.

Fun fact about Maliha: She can go on for days just on coffee and noodles... daaaaays. true story!
